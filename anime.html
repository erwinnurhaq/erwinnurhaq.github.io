Synopsis:

Kiriyama Rei lost his family since childhood and adopted by a relative family. There, Kiriyama learns about Shogi from his step father who lives as a pro Shogi player. But due to enermous amount of pressure both from Shogi community and his step sister in his new family, he seeks indepence from his complex emotions by moving and living on his own. Continuing his own life just from Shogi.
